Swimming is fun. For some it is a way to find inner peace and to some it is a magical way to stay fit. Whatever it may be, we must acknowledge that it can sometimes save lives too. Often in news we hear that someone died because of boat drowing or sometimes it is because of mild loss of focus. Surprisingly, many of these cases are mostly related to childrens.
This is why learning to swim should be an integral part of our lives. Here at J.Y.S. Foundation, you will find it much easier to learn swimming with our proven methods.

PROTECTION

"People protect what they love." - Jacques Yves Cousteau

We believe that life is outmost precious and hence J.Y.S. Foundation have adequete amount of lifebuoys! And not only lifebuoys, we also have sufficient life-jackets ready to be used. We are the only one in this area to provide such an extreme level of protection. So that you and your family can have peace of mind, while you are in our water.

TECHNIQUES

"All I have to do is to work on transition and technique." - Usain Bolt.

Here at J.Y.S. Foundation, we have developed an amazing technique of touchless teaching towards female candidates. Using this process swimming tutor doesn't need to touch the female candidate. Apart from that, our technique is so advanced that anyone can learn to swim within one week!! and atmost it might take one month! Hence, with this technique, you can be assured that any non-swimmer would learn to swim within few weeks.

ENTERTAINMENT & FUN

"Play is our brain’s favorite way of learning." - Diane Ackerman.

With swimming, we also provide water volley ball games for our students. It is a very intuitive way to learn how to stay afloat in water and move vigorously. It rejuvenates whole body. Not only water volley is fun but it makes our students stronger. With Volleyball we also have optional Archery and Kayaking options. There are occasional competetions for our students to compete against each others. And the good news is that, this isn't all! there are surprises as well :)
